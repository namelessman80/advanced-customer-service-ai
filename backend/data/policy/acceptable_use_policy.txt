Acceptable Use Policy

This Acceptable Use Policy ("AUP") governs your use of our service and defines prohibited activities that may result in suspension or termination of your account. By using the service, you agree to comply with this policy. We reserve the right to investigate violations and take appropriate action, including cooperation with law enforcement.

Prohibited Uses and Activities: You may not use the service to engage in or facilitate any of the following prohibited activities: (1) Illegal Activities - violating any applicable laws, regulations, or legal obligations; engaging in fraud, money laundering, terrorism, or other criminal activities; accessing, storing, or distributing child sexual abuse material (CSAM) or other illegal content; violating intellectual property rights, including copyright, trademark, or patent infringement; harassment, stalking, or threatening others; facilitating or promoting illegal drug sales or other black market activities. (2) Abuse and Harassment - harassing, bullying, threatening, or intimidating other users or third parties; publishing or sharing "doxing" content (private personal information published without consent); creating or distributing revenge pornography or non-consensual intimate images; impersonating others or creating fake accounts to deceive users; coordinating hate speech, violence, or discrimination against protected groups; engaging in targeted campaigns to disrupt other users' experience. (3) Security Violations - attempting unauthorized access to the service, other users' accounts, or our systems; introducing viruses, malware, ransomware, or other malicious code; conducting security scans, penetration tests, or vulnerability assessments without written authorization; using the service to launch denial-of-service (DoS) attacks or distribute attack tools; bypassing or circumventing security features, authentication mechanisms, or access controls; sharing, selling, or trading your account credentials or API keys; reverse engineering, decompiling, or disassembling our software. (4) Spam and Abuse - sending unsolicited commercial emails, spam, or bulk messages through the service; scraping, crawling, or harvesting data from the service beyond what's permitted by our API; creating fake accounts for spamming, manipulation, or deception; artificially inflating metrics, engagement, or activity through bots or automated tools; using the service to operate email lists or bulk sending services without proper consent; engaging in phishing, social engineering, or deceptive practices. (5) Resource Abuse - excessive or abusive use of system resources that degrades service for others; circumventing rate limits or usage quotas through technical means; operating cryptocurrency mining operations using our infrastructure; reselling or white-labeling the service without authorization; using the service to provide similar services to competitors; storing excessive data unrelated to legitimate business purposes. (6) Objectionable Content - hosting or distributing content that is defamatory, obscene, or harmful; promoting violence, self-harm, or dangerous activities; sharing graphic violence or disturbing content without appropriate warnings; distributing misleading or deceptive information with intent to harm; hosting or facilitating gambling, betting, or gaming services in prohibited jurisdictions; promoting regulated substances or services without proper licensing.

Enforcement and Violations: We employ automated systems and human review to detect violations of this AUP. When a violation is identified, we may take the following actions depending on severity: Warning - first-time minor violations may result in a warning notice with a request to correct the behavior. Content Removal - violating content will be removed or quarantined with notification to you. Temporary Suspension - serious or repeated violations may result in temporary account suspension (7-30 days) with restricted access. Permanent Termination - severe violations (illegal content, security breaches, repeat offenses) result in permanent account termination without refund. Legal Action - we may report violations to law enforcement, cooperate with investigations, and preserve evidence as required by law. Financial Penalties - violations that cause financial harm may result in billing for damages, legal fees, and incident response costs. Appeals - if you believe enforcement action was taken in error, you may appeal by contacting appeals@yourservice.com with a detailed explanation and evidence. We review appeals within 7 business days.

Reporting Violations: If you become aware of content or activities that violate this AUP, please report them immediately: Report Button - use the "Report" button available on content within the service. Email - send detailed reports to abuse@yourservice.com with subject line "AUP Violation Report." Include: URL or location of violating content, description of the violation, relevant screenshots or evidence (if safe and legal to capture), your contact information for follow-up (optional but helpful). We investigate all reports within 48 hours and will take appropriate action. You may report anonymously, but providing contact information helps us investigate effectively. We do not tolerate retaliation against users who report violations in good faith.
